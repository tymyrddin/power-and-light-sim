# Security research exception

Notwithstanding the [Polyform Noncommercial License](LICENSE), the author explicitly permits the use of this software 
for legitimate security research purposes.

## Permitted activities

The following activities are explicitly allowed without a commercial license, provided they are conducted in good faith:

- Vulnerability research and analysis
- Development of proof-of-concept exploits
- Defensive research and mitigation testing
- Red team / blue team exercises where no fee is charged
- Academic research and publication
- Incident response preparation and tabletop exercises
- Demonstrations intended to improve security awareness

This includes interaction with the simulator over real network interfaces, protocol fuzzing, and external attack tooling.

## Responsible use

Users are expected to:

- Operate within environments they own or have permission to test
- Follow responsible disclosure practices where applicable
- Avoid causing harm outside controlled environments

## What this exception does not allow

This exception does not permit:

- Paid security services
- Commercial penetration testing
- Consultancy engagements
- Training delivered for a fee

If your research transitions into a paid engagement, a commercial license is required.

## Intent

The intent of this exception is to raise the level of security, not to restrict it. If you believe your use case 
aligns with this intent but falls into a grey area, [reach out](https://tymyrddin.dev/contact/).
